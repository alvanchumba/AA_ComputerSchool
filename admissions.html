U.A. High School - Admissions

Application Process

Admission to U.A. High School is highly competitive, and applicants must demonstrate exceptional abilities and potential as heroes. The application process includes several steps:

  1. Complete the online application form, which includes personal and academic information, as well as a personal essay.
  2. Submit academic transcripts and test scores, as well as any relevant extracurricular activities and achievements.
  3. Pass a preliminary screening, which includes a written exam and a physical fitness test.
  4. Attend an interview with faculty and staff, where applicants will be asked to demonstrate their hero skills and discuss their goals and aspirations.

The admissions committee carefully reviews each application and selects only the most qualified and promising candidates.

Requirements

Applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 15 years old
  • Have a quirk that demonstrates potential for hero work
  • Pass a criminal background check
  • Meet minimum academic and physical fitness standards

Please note that meeting these requirements does not guarantee admission, as the competition for spots is extremely high.

Important Dates

The admissions process for the upcoming academic year typically begins in the fall of the previous year, and includes the following dates:

  • Application deadline: December 31st
  • Preliminary screening: January - February
  • Interviews: March - April
  • Admissions decisions: May 1st

Please check the U.A. High School website regularly for updates and announcements regarding the admissions process.
