Hero Training Course

The Hero Training course is designed to help students develop their quirk and hone their skills to become professional heroes. The course covers a range of topics, including:

  • Quirk Training
  • Combat Techniques
  • Rescue Operations
  • Hero Ethics
Learn More

Quirk Control Course

The Quirk Control course focuses on helping students understand and control their quirk. Students learn:

  • Quirk Anatomy
  • Quirk Limitations
  • Quirk Amplification
  • Quirk Combination
Learn More

Battle Strategy Course

The Battle Strategy course teaches students how to strategize and plan for battles, taking into account their own strengths and weaknesses, as well as those of their opponents. Topics covered include:

  • Tactics and Maneuvers
  • Analysis of Opponents
  • Equipment and Gear
  • Team Coordination
Learn More

Villain Rehabilitation Course

The Villain Rehabilitation course is designed for students who want to understand the motivations and behaviors of villains, and help them turn over a new leaf. The course covers:

  • Understanding Villains
  • Rehabilitation Techniques
  • Post-Rehabilitation Support
  • Dealing with Relapses
